Website Design for 1800's Themed Hotel

Individual Project | Duration : 2 weeks

Design Goal: To be able to design a relatively complex graphic user interface, and be able to provide a valid design rationale for it.

This was an academic project done for a small hotel outside of Gothenburg called WÃ¤rdshuset Spinnaren. We had have two chances to meet the client: the buyer of the house who had a new concept for the hotel. The idea was to turn the house into a time capsule: the guests will get to wear clothes from the 1800s and try the food of the time. For this, she needed a new room booking system which will include some unusual data for a hotel, such as clothing sizes of the guests, etc. The features and requirements were quite specific. Moreover data like hotel brochure, logo and photos were provided by the client.
